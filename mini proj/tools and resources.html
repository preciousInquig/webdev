Applications & Websites to Study Easier

Apps

  • Notion

    A versatile workspace that combines notes, tasks, databases, and calendars.

    How-to:

    1. Create a dashboard to organize your study materials.
    2. Use templates for class notes, assignments, and reading lists.
    3. Integrate with Google Calendar for scheduling.
  • TickTick

    A task manager with to-do lists, calendars, and a Pomodoro timer.

    How-to:

    1. Set daily study goals and break them into tasks.
    2. Use the Pomodoro timer to maintain focus.
    3. Review progress weekly to adjust goals.
  • TaskRocky

    A gamified productivity app that combines task management with rewards.

    How-to:

    1. Create tasks and assign points for completion.
    2. Earn rewards as you accumulate points.
    3. Use the app to stay motivated and track progress.

Websites

  • Coursera

    Offers online courses from universities and companies worldwide.

    How-to:

    1. Browse courses by subject or university.
    2. Enroll in courses to supplement your studies.
    3. Earn certificates to enhance your resume.
  • EdX

    Provides high-quality online courses from top institutions.

    How-to:

    1. Select courses based on your academic interests.
    2. Participate in discussions and assignments.
    3. Apply learned concepts to your studies.
  • Grammarly

    An AI-powered writing assistant that helps with grammar, clarity, and style.

    How-to:

    1. Install the browser extension or use the web app.
    2. Write or paste your text into the editor.
    3. Review suggestions and improve your writing.

Planners & Templates

  • Canva Student Planner Templates

    Customizable templates for study schedules, to-do lists, and goal tracking.

    How-to:

    1. Browse the collection of student planner templates.
    2. Select a template that suits your needs.
    3. Personalize it with your study schedule and tasks.
  • Passion Planner Free Downloads

    Free printable planners to help organize your study and personal life.

    How-to:

    1. Visit the free downloads section.
    2. Choose a planner that fits your style.
    3. Download and print it for use.
  • SmartGirl Study Printables

    Variety of study printables, including timetables and weekly planners.

    How-to:

    1. Explore the free downloads section.
    2. Select the printables you need.
    3. Print and use them to organize your study sessions.
