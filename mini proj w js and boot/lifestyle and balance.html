Lifestyle & Balance

Importance of Sleep

  • Sleep and Academic Performance

    • Adequate sleep is crucial for memory and focus.

  • Sleep Hygiene Tips

    • Limit screen time, avoid caffeine, and maintain a routine.

  • Impact of Sleep Deprivation

    • Lack of sleep can lead to poor concentration and mood swings.

Healthy Snacks for Focus

  • Nuts & Seeds — omega-3s that boost brain power.
  • Dark Chocolate — improves focus and memory.
  • Fruit + Protein Combos — balanced energy for studying.
