Applications & Websites to Study Easier

Apps

  • Notion — A versatile workspace that combines notes, tasks, databases, and calendars.

    • How-to:

    • Create a dashboard to organize your study materials.
    • Use templates for class notes, assignments, and reading lists.
    • Integrate with Google Calendar for scheduling.
  • TickTick — A task manager that includes to-do lists, calendars, and a Pomodoro timer.

    • How-to:

    • Set daily study goals and break them into tasks.
    • Use the Pomodoro timer to maintain focus.
    • Review your progress weekly to adjust goals.
  • TaskRocky — A gamified productivity app that combines task management with rewards.

    • How-to:

    • Create tasks and assign points for completion.
    • Earn rewards as you accumulate points.
    • Use the app to stay motivated and track progress.

Websites

  • Coursera — Offers online courses from universities and companies worldwide.

    • How-to:

    • Browse courses by subject or university.
    • Enroll in courses to supplement your studies.
    • Earn certificates to enhance your resume.

    Watch Video Tutorial: Free video courses – Short introduction to Coursera

  • EdX — Provides high-quality online courses from top institutions.

    • How-to:

    • Select courses based on your academic interests.
    • Participate in discussions and assignments.
    • Apply learned concepts to your studies.

    Watch Video Tutorial: EdX Introduction

  • Grammarly — An AI-powered writing assistant that helps with grammar, clarity, and style.

    • How-to:

    • Install the browser extension or use the web app.
    • Write or paste your text into the editor.
    • Review suggestions and improve your writing.

Planners & Templates

  • Canva Student Planner Templates — Customizable templates for study schedules, to-do lists, and goal tracking.

    • How-to:

    • Browse the collection of student planner templates.
    • Select a template that suits your needs.
    • Personalize it with your study schedule and tasks.
  • Passion Planner Free Downloads — Offers free printable planners to help organize your study and personal life.

    • How-to:

    • Visit the free downloads section.
    • Choose a planner that fits your style.
    • Download and print it for use.
  • SmartGirl Study Printables — Provides a variety of study printables, including timetables and weekly planners.

    • How-to:

    • Explore the free downloads section.
    • Select the printables you need.
    • Print and use them to organize your study sessions.
