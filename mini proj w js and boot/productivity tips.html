Productivity Tips

Time Management

  • Eisenhower Matrix.

    How-to:

    1. Draw a 2x2 grid.
    2. Label axes as "Urgent" and "Important."
    3. Categorize tasks into four quadrants: Do First, Schedule, Delegate, and Don't Do

  • Pomodoro Technique

    How-to:

    1. Set a timer for 25 minutes (one Pomodoro).
    2. Work on a task until the timer rings.
    3. Take a 5-minute break.
    4. After four Pomodoros, take a longer break (15–30 minutes).

  • Time Blocking

    How-to:

    1. Use a calendar or planner.
    2. Block out time for focused work, meetings, and breaks.
    3. Stick to the schedule as much as possible.

Beating Procrastination

  • The 2-Minute Rule

    How-to:

    1. Identify tasks that can be completed quickly.
    2. Complete them right away to reduce mental clutter.
    3. Use this strategy to overcome resistance to larger tasks.

  • Self-Compassion

    How-to:

    1. Acknowledge feelings of frustration or failure without judgment.
    2. Encourage yourself as you would a friend.
    3. Use positive self-talk to motivate action.

  • Break Tasks into Smaller Steps.

    How-to:

    1. Identify the overall task.
    2. Break it down (choose a topic → make an outline → write intro → draft body → edit).
    3. Focus on finishing one step at a time.
    4. Celebrate small wins to keep momentum going.
